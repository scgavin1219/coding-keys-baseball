Goal
  • Try to hit as many homeruns as you can before you get three outs.
  • Stop the stopwatch between 0.90 and 1.00 seconds to hit a homerun
  • Any other result is an out!
    How to Play
  • Click the mouse to start the stopwatch
  • Click the mouse to stop the stopwatch
  • When you are ready for next round, click the next round button
  • HIT BOMBS!
